# Creatures

SimplyFellas integrates a wide variety of creature mods that add new animals, overhaul existing mobs, and enhance creature-related gameplay. Some mods overhaul the creatures you already know, while others add entirely new ones, creating a more diverse and interesting world.

## About

This modpack includes various useful mods such as Butchercraft and Tide, which provide many kinds of foods and farming methods. This pack also has many mods that add additional creatures and bosses, making exploration more exciting and rewarding. From adorable pets to dangerous new mobs, the creature mods in SimplyFellas add depth and variety to your Minecraft experience.

---

## New Creatures

### Critters and Companions

This mod adds various different creatures to your world such as otters, koi fish, red pandas, and more! These friendly creatures add life and charm to your world.

<img src="Otters.png" alt="Otters" class="inline-image">
<img src="Koi.png" alt="Koi Fish" class="inline-image">

### Adorable Hamster Pets

Adds hamsters you can tame with sliced cucumbers. Hamsters can forage for diamonds and more, making them both cute and useful companions!

<img src="HamsterPets.png" alt="Hamster Pets" class="inline-image">

### Ribbits

This mod adds new frog villages in the swamp biomes with their own frog villagers. Explore swamp biomes to find these unique settlements!

<img src="Ribbits.png" alt="Ribbits" class="inline-image">

### Wandering Ribbit

Adds a new wandering ribbit trader that you can encounter while exploring, offering unique trades and interactions.

---

## Creature Overhauls

### Creeper Overhaul

This mod overhauls the vanilla creepers to have various different types and effects. Different biomes spawn different creeper variants, each with unique behaviors and appearances!

<img src="OceanCreeper.png" alt="Ocean Creeper" class="inline-image">

### More Axolotl Variants

This mod overhauls the vanilla axolotls to have various different types. Discover new axolotl variants with unique colors and patterns!

---

## Fishing & Aquatic Life

### Tide

This mod heavily expands upon fishing, offering 60+ new types of fish as well as bait and bobbers. Fishing becomes a much more diverse and rewarding activity with tons of new catches!

<img src="FishingInCave.png" alt="Cave Fishing" class="inline-image">

## Trading & Interactions

### Goblin Traders

This mod adds goblin traders you can find underground or in the nether. These unique traders offer interesting items and provide new trading opportunities in unexpected locations!

<img src="GoblinTraders.png" alt="Goblin Traders" class="inline-image">

---

## Food & Butchering

### Butchercraft

This mod adds butchering to Minecraft. There are meat hooks, cleavers, and ways to turn mobs into various different foods. This adds depth to food preparation and makes mob drops more useful!

<img src="Butchercraft.png" alt="Butchercraft" class="inline-image">

---

## Enhanced Loot & Items

### Naturally Trimmed

This mod alters the vanilla loot tables for both spawned mobs and generated loot to include pre-trimmed armors and tools. Find naturally trimmed equipment with unique appearances!

<img src="Naturally Trimmed.png" alt="Naturally Trimmed" class="inline-image">

### More Dragon Eggs

This mod changes the ender dragon to drop a dragon egg upon every death, instead of just the first time you beat it. This allows you to collect multiple dragon eggs for your builds!
