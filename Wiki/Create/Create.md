# Create

Create is an engineering mod that powers automations and brings a Minecraft-flavored factory aspect to your game! What you can create is entirely up to your imagination! SimplyFellas features an extensive collection of Create mods and addons that expand the automation possibilities far beyond the base mod.

## About

Create enhances various aspects of your Minecraft experience by allowing you to automate almost everything you can do! Create brings tons of expansive content to the table, and we have carefully crafted a huge selection of Create mods to make your experience that much more immersive! From core automation to advanced electricity systems, food processing to transportation, the Create ecosystem in SimplyFellas offers nearly unlimited automation possibilities.

The modpack includes over 30 Create-related mods covering automation, decoration, integration, performance optimization, and much more. Whether you're building simple contraptions or complex automated factories, this collection provides all the tools you need.

## Core Create Mod

### Create

Create is THE automation mod that fits the vanilla aesthetic of Minecraft! Craft your own factories using various methods of generating rotational power! You can even create self-sufficient train networks to transport your goods! The mod provides a comprehensive automation system built around rotational force, allowing you to build complex contraptions and automated systems.

## Major Expansions

### Create: The Factory Must Grow

Expands Create to the age of diesel and electricity with massive diesel engines, powerlines, oil refining, additional materials, and even more! This is one of the largest Create expansions, adding industrial-scale automation.

<img src="Refinery.png" alt="Factory Must Grow Refinery" class="inline-image">

### Create: Diesel Generators

Expands the oil refining aspect of Create! Adds more engines, upgrades, fuels, molding/casting, burners, and even more! Complements The Factory Must Grow with additional power generation options.

### Create: Crafts & Additions

Adds electricity and wires to the Create mod! Introduces electrical systems that can work alongside or in place of rotational force.

### Create: New Age

Integrates various multiblock structures with electricity generation, wires, heat, and more. Adds advanced multiblock automation systems.

### Create: Ultimate Factory

Brings way more automatable recipes to the Create mod and allows you to automate almost every aspect of Minecraft! Greatly expands what can be automated.

## Food & Processing

### Create: Food

Brings hundreds of new food items to Create along with their own automatable recipes and integrations with Farmer's Delight!

### Create: Confectionary

Brings chocolate factories to Create! Allows you to create cocoa, butter, caramel, chocolate, marshmallow, and even more! Perfect for building automated confectionary production lines.

### Create: Slice & Dice

Integrates Farmer's Delight into automatable recipes with Create! Allows you to automate food processing from Farmer's Delight.

### Create: Central Kitchen

Further integrates Farmer's Delight as well as Extra Delight into even more automatable recipes with Create! Expands food automation even further.

### Create: Dragons Plus

Offers more processing methods with Create such as bulk coloring, freezing, ending, and sanding! Adds additional processing capabilities.

<img src="Processing.png" alt="Create Processing" class="inline-image">

## Utilities & Tools

### Create: Stuff N' Additions

Offers a brand new set of equipment, tools, and gadgets to the Create mod! Includes jetpacks, magnets, drills, and tons more! Adds useful tools and equipment that integrate with Create systems.

### Create: Stuff N' Additions Tank Fix

Creates four new accessory slots for your tanks and integrates tanks with Create spouts and Sophisticated Backpacks!

### Create: Curios Jetpack & Backtank

Allows you to use the backtank or jetpack from Stuff N' Additions with the back accessory slot!

### Create: Hypertubes

Adds rotation-powered hypertubes to allow you to zip around your factory with ease! You can even use it to suck in fish or villagers!

<img src="Fish Hypertube.gif" alt="Create Hypertubes" class="inline-image">

### Create: Power Loader

Adds immersive chunkloaders to the Create mod! Powered by rotational force and created using captured ghasts!

### Create: Mechanical Spawner

A rotation-powered mechanical spawner that generates mobs using spawn fluid!

### Create: Ultimine

Allows you to ultimine Create blocks with the Create wrench!

### Create: Enchantable Machinery

This mod allows you to enchant the Create blocks, such as using Silk Touch with a mechanical drill!

### Create: Enchantment Industry

An expansive mod that adds various tools and methods to handle experience and enchantments using the Create mod! This includes liquid XP, blaze enchanters, blaze forgers, mechanical grinders, and tons more!

---

## Villagers & Trading

### Create: Better Villager

Adds a plethora of Create-themed villager professions using Create blocks as workstations! That's right, you can trade Create items with your villagers!

### Create: Trading Floor

Adds the trading depot block which allows you to automate villager trading with the Create mod!

### Create: Broken Bad

Adds a breaking bad aspect to Create! Yes, you heard me right! You can use various scientific methods using chemicals to formulate "The Blue Stuff" and trade it with your villagers!

---

## Decoration & Building

### Create: Copycats+

Brings tons more copycat blocks to the Create mod which allows you to create essentially any variant of any block you can imagine! Get ready to build away!

<img src="Copycats.png" alt="Create Copycats" class="inline-image">

### Create: Bells & Whistles

Adds adornments and decorations for use in Create contraptions and builds.

### Rechiseled: Create

Integrates the create materials into the rechiseled mod for even more decorative options.

## World Generation

### Create: Structures Arise

A world generation mod which adds 20 new Create-themed structures including ships, towers, islands, and more!

---

## Performance & Optimization

### Create: Smart Bounds

Optimizes the Create mod's block entity renderbounds. Improves performance when using many Create blocks.

### Create: Better FPS

Improves your FPS while using shader packs with Create.

### Colorwheel

Optimizes Iris shaders with Flywheel (Create's backend mod).

### Colorwheel Patcher

Patches various shader packs to work with Flywheel.

---

## Integrations

### Create: Contraption Terminals

Allows Tom's Simple Storage Terminals to work with Create contraptions!

### CC:C Bridge

Adds more peripherals between Create and CC: Tweaked (ComputerCraft).

### Create: Better CC Recipes

Balances ComputerCraft by integrating its recipes with the Create mod and its resources.

### Create: CC Total Logistics

Allows more flexible CC: Tweaked logistics management through peripherals.

### Create: Liquid Fuel

Allows you to directly pump liquid fuel into blaze burners.
